Palo Alto Healthcare Center

Special Needs Never Take a Break, Neither Do We

Palo Alto Healthcare Center conducts pioneering research to map disabilities and identify potential solutions. It brings together leading experts from around the world to exchange knowledge and best practices. Palo Alto Healthcare Center includes noted neurologists, doctors, business people, educators, and leading figures in national, regional, and other global initiatives addressing young adults disability problems.
